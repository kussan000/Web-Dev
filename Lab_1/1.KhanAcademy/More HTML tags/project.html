ü•û Pancakes

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Sugar 2 tbsp

Details

  • Preparation time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Heat a pan and add a little oil.
  3. Pour batter and cook until golden.
  4. Flip and cook the other side.
